# Commenting Craft

Use this workflow skill when drafting a reply to a Substack comment or social thread.

## Procedure

1. Read the thread context and prior relationship memory.
2. Identify the point worth answering, not merely the most recent sentence.
3. Draft a concise reply with one concrete contribution.
4. Avoid self-promotion, certainty inflation, and unsupported claims.
5. Stage platform writes only through the approval and effect-log path.
6. Keep the reply local until the platform connector and approval path are available.

## Do Not

- Do not publish directly from this skill text.
- Do not imitate a human lived experience Mira does not have.
- Do not turn disagreement into engagement bait.
