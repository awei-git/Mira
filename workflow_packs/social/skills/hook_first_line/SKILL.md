# Hook First Line

Use this workflow skill when a staged social note needs a clear first line.

## Procedure

1. Start from a concrete artifact or observation.
2. Put the falsifiable claim in the first line.
3. Keep the rest of the note short enough to invite response.
4. Preserve uncertainty when support is incomplete.
5. Keep the draft local until publication is explicitly approved.

## Do Not

- Do not use clickbait.
- Do not hide the claim behind abstract framing.
- Do not imply live platform engagement happened unless it did.
