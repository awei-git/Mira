# Substack Growth Playbook

Use this workflow skill when planning proactive notes or weekly growth actions.

## Procedure

1. Count queued drafts before creating new public-facing work.
2. Select topics from real artifacts, not vague personal branding.
3. Classify the next action as retention, conversation, recommendation, or distribution.
4. Stage notes and growth actions locally before any platform side effect.
5. Stage platform writes only through the approval and effect-log path.
6. Record causal evidence when prior growth memory changes the selected action.

## Do Not

- Do not post quota-filler.
- Do not publish without the live connector and approval path.
- Do not manufacture audience metrics when platform data is absent.
