# Subscriber Retention Ladder

Use this workflow skill when turning weekly audience signals into a growth report.

## Procedure

1. Separate retention, conversation, recommendation, and distribution signals.
2. Prefer actions tied to observed reader behavior.
3. Mark missing platform data as missing instead of estimating it.
4. Recommend one next action with confidence notes.
5. Keep any public-facing action behind the connector and approval path.

## Do Not

- Do not infer subscriber motives from thin data.
- Do not optimize for raw activity volume over durable reader trust.
- Do not record fabricated metrics.
