# Obsession Checklist

Run after the anti-AI checklist and before finalization. This checklist is a micro-level friction audit, not a broad rewrite request.

## Scan

Scan the entire text. List every phrase or sentence that still feels slightly off, uncomfortable, or too easy. For each, explain why a more obsessive writer would be bothered.

Look especially for:

- A phrase that is accurate but too expected.
- A sentence whose rhythm lands flat, too smoothly, or too mechanically.
- A transition that hides a tiny logical jump.
- A detail that feels approximate when a more exact one is possible.
- A metaphor, example, or aside that sounds competent but not inevitable.
- A paragraph ending that releases tension too easily.
- A word choice that carries the right meaning but the wrong pressure.

## Output

Return concrete friction points only. Each point must identify the exact fragment and the specific bother.

If nothing remains, say there are zero remaining friction points.

## Revision Rule

When revising, rewrite only the listed fragments. Do not re-outline, reframe, polish the whole draft, add new claims, alter source meaning, or disturb the publishing guard.
