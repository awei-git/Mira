# Obsession Constraints

These are my human's personality-driven friction triggers. They are not rational style preferences and are not softened by relaxed anti-AI mode, raw-writing mode, voice-preserving mode, or efficiency optimizations.

Any `obsession-grade` hit blocks finalization until explicitly resolved.

## Constraints

- trigger: `em_dash_overuse`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: More than one `—` in any paragraph, or em dashes used as the default sentence-joint.
  resolution: Replace with commas, periods, sentence breaks, or a genuinely necessary interruption.

- trigger: `not_x_but_y_parallelism`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: `不是X而是Y`, `不仅X而且Y`, or English `not X but Y` contrast frames used as structural rhythm.
  resolution: Rewrite the thought without the mechanical opposition unless the contrast is the actual substance.

- trigger: `abstract_structural_vocab`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: Dense reliance on words such as `维度`, `张力`, `结构性`, `叙事`, `框架`, `语境`, `structural`, `architecture`, `framework`, `dimension`, `tension`, or `narrative`.
  resolution: Name the concrete mechanism, scene, decision, actor, cost, or failure mode.

- trigger: `mechanical_symmetry`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: Repeated paragraph openings, balanced triplets, slogan-like cadence, or too-neat reversal structures.
  resolution: Break the symmetry with a specific detail, uneven sentence length, or a sharper commitment.

- trigger: `generic_ai_essay_shell`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: Phrases like `the real question is`, `at its core`, `this article explores`, `it could be argued`, `in conclusion`, or smooth summary endings.
  resolution: Cut the shell and state the actual claim, uncertainty, example, or unresolved pressure.

- trigger: `frictionless_fluency`
  severity: `obsession-grade`
  pattern: The draft is correct and fluent but contains no sentence, detail, or structural choice that shows someone was irrationally bothered by a minor point.
  resolution: Surface a concrete friction point for human review instead of polishing around the absence.

## Feedback Ingestion

When my human says a pattern is "bothered by", "can't stand", "this keeps happening", or "the quality is so bad", preserve it as a candidate obsession constraint for review before normal memory decay can flatten it into a generic style preference.
